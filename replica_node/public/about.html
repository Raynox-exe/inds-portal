About the Institute

Championing the developmental and cultural interests of the Niger Delta region.

Our History & Mission

The Institute for Niger Delta Studies (INDS) was established to serve as a high-level research and advocacy center within Niger Delta University. Our mission is to promote the sustainable development of the Niger Delta through rigorous academic research, policy documentation, and community engagement.

We believe that the challenges of the region—environment, economic equity, and social justice—require intellectual solutions grounded in the local context and cultural realities of our people.

Institutional Objectives

  • To conduct and coordinate research into the history, culture, and environment of the Niger Delta.
  • To provide a platform for documentation and preservation of indigenous knowledge systems.
  • To organize conferences, seminars, and memorial lectures (like the Melford Okilo series).
  • To publish the Research Digest, a peer-reviewed academic journal of regional importance.
