Guidelines and portal access for scholarly contributions to the Research Digest.

Call for Articles

The Institute for Niger Delta Studies welcomes manuscripts for its upcoming Research Digest volumes. Submissions must be original, peer-reviewed contributions addressing regional themes, policy analysis, and developmental studies.

Next Deadline September 30th, 2024
Peer Review Timeline 4-6 Weeks

Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts: 4,000 to 7,000 words.
  • Abstract: Maximum 300 words.
  • Referencing: APA 7th Edition.
  • Format: .DOC, .DOCX, or .PDF.

Ready to Submit?

You must have an active researcher account to submit manuscripts through our secure portal.
